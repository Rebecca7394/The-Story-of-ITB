Meet past student James McGuirk

About James

James is a past student from ITB and has been grabbing every opertuniety since! James studied Creative Digital Media and his favourite module was design. Since graduating from ITB James has been very successful in the design industry creating posters for various dance music event and working for District Magazine as their designer. James found his click while studying at ITB and continues to persue his passion in the design industry.

Interview with James

  1. How did your time at ITB benefit you in your work?

    2. When I first heard of the Creative Digital Media course at ITB, I knew it was something I was going to really enjoy. Academic courses weren't for me, I was creative and expessed myself best in this field. I went into the course with an open mind, I wasn't particulary talented at any module but was keen to learn. When I started doing design in college something really clicked, I found my feet and began designing in my free time. Knowing a lot of people in the music industry I was given the opertunity to design the promotion for their nights. After a year or two I was then hired as designer for the District Magazine and District nights. If it wasn't for ITB I might have never found my calling in the design industry.

  2. What was your favourite part of attending ITB?

    3. Learning new skills and being taught by passionate lectures, getting to know new people with simular interests as you and enjoying the college experience.

  3. What life qualties and skills have you gained from attending ITB?

    4. Not to be afraid to ask for help, people are there to help you! I matured during my time at ITB and found my feet. I gained a lot of social skills from meeting tones of new people and came out of my shell.
