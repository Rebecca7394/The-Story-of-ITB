Meet future student Megan Nolan

About Megan

Megan is a current student in Luteralstown Community College. As it is her final year in school she has been looking at her options for next year. She is passionate about photography and is interested in studying Creative Digital Media at ITB when she finished secondary school. She is worried about the transition from secondary school to college and hope the work load isn't overbareing.


Interview with Megan

  1. Are you looking forward to the transition between secondary school and college?

    2. Im excited, but it is also nerve wrecking to think about it. I think it will take me awhile to get my head around being treated like an adult rather than being instructed by a teacher. Im really looking forward to the college experience as my friends who are in college have a lot of fun around campus and on college nights out. Im really looking forward to studying something that I find interesting, I tend to lose a lot of focus in school when its a subject that doesn't appeal to me.

  2. What qualties would you like your future college to have?

    3. A fun, active campus with a variety of activies that take place for students to get involved. Nice staff who are passionate about what they teach. Acess to computers and equipment in college and a nice canteen, the ones in secondary school are terrible!

  3. Why would you consider studying at ITB in the future?

    4. After attending the open day and getting an insight to the college and the courses avilable at ITB, I really liked the layout and structor of the college. I love photography and after hearing about the Creative Digital Media course, it seemed right up my ally! The students and staff were really helpful when I had a question.
